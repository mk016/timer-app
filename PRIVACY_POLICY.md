# Privacy Policy for Aura Timer

**Effective Date:** July 22, 2026

Welcome to **Aura Timer** ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application **Aura Timer** (the "App").

---

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**Aura Timer does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII).**

- **Local Storage:** All app data, including custom timer presets, accent color preferences, and sound settings, is saved exclusively on your local device using standard local storage (`AsyncStorage`). This data never leaves your device.
- **No User Accounts:** You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details (such as name, email, or phone number) to use Aura Timer.

---

### 2. Device Permissions

Aura Timer requests minimal permissions necessary for core app functionality:

- **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`):** Used solely to alert you when your active timer or countdown has completed. Notification processing occurs entirely on your device.
- **Audio & Haptics:** Used locally to play completion chimes and provide haptic vibration feedback.
- **Keep Awake:** Used to prevent the screen from dimming while a timer is actively running in full-screen mode.

We do NOT access your contacts, camera, microphone, precise location, or photos.

---

### 3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

- **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics) or advertising networks.
- **No Ads:** Aura Timer is completely ad-free and does not send any telemetry or usage metrics to external servers.

---

### 4. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

---

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
